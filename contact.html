Frequently Asked Questions

What should I bring for my dive or snorkeling trip?

We recommend bringing: swimwear, towel, sunscreen, hat, sunglasses, and a change of clothes. For diving, bring your certification card if you're certified. We provide all diving and snorkeling equipment. Don't forget your camera!

Is there an age limit for diving or snorkeling?

For scuba diving: Minimum age for Discover Scuba is 10 years. For PADI Open Water certification, minimum age is 10-14 for Junior Open Water and 15+ for regular certification. For snorkeling: Children as young as 5 can join with parental supervision and proper flotation devices.

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellations made 48 hours or more before the scheduled activity receive a full refund. Cancellations made 24-48 hours before receive a 50% refund. Cancellations made less than 24 hours before are non-refundable. In case of bad weather, we'll reschedule or provide a full refund.

Do I need to know how to swim to dive or snorkel?

For snorkeling: Basic swimming ability is recommended but not required. We provide floatation devices and can accommodate non-swimmers in shallow, calm areas. For scuba diving: You don't need to be a strong swimmer, but you should be comfortable in the water. We'll assess your comfort level during the briefing.

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least 2-3 days in advance, especially during peak season (June-September, December-January). For certification courses, book at least one week in advance. Last-minute bookings may be possible depending on availability.

What medical conditions prevent diving?

Certain medical conditions may prevent diving, including: heart conditions, lung diseases, epilepsy, recent surgeries, pregnancy, and severe asthma. All participants must complete a medical questionnaire. If you have any concerns, consult with your doctor before booking.

Do you offer transportation from hotels?

Yes, we offer complimentary transportation from hotels in the Dar es Salaam city center and Masaki area. For hotels outside this area, we can arrange transportation for a small additional fee. Please mention your hotel location when booking.
