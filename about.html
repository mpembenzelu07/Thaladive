Our Story & Mission

Discover the passion behind Thalassa Dive Center and meet the team dedicated to creating unforgettable underwater experiences in Tanzania

Our Story

Founded in 2015 by marine biologist Ali Hassan and PADI Master Instructor Sarah Johnson, Thalassa Dive Center began with a simple mission: to share the incredible underwater world of Tanzania with visitors from around the globe while promoting ocean conservation.

What started as a small operation with one boat and two instructors has grown into Dar es Salaam's premier PADI-certified dive center. Over the years, we've certified thousands of divers, led countless guided dives, and built a reputation for safety, professionalism, and unforgettable experiences.

Our name "Thalassa" comes from the Greek word for sea, reflecting our deep connection to the ocean and our commitment to marine conservation. We believe that by helping people experience the beauty of the underwater world, we inspire them to protect it.

Excellence

We strive for excellence in everything we do, from customer service to dive experiences, ensuring every guest leaves with amazing memories.

Safety & Certifications

At Thalassa Dive Center, safety is our top priority. We maintain the highest standards in dive safety and hold numerous certifications and accreditations.

All our instructors are PADI certified with additional specialties in rescue, deep diving, and emergency first response. We maintain a maximum 4:1 student-to-instructor ratio for personalized attention and safety.

Our equipment is serviced monthly by certified technicians, and we carry comprehensive insurance for all our operations. We're proud to be recognized as one of Tanzania's safest and most professional dive operations.
